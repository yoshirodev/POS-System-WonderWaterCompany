About Us

Wender Water Equipment and Supplies Trading is a trusted medium-scale water equipment supplier based in Naga City, proudly providing 80% of Bicol’s water equipment and supply needs, serving walk-in customers daily and supplying majority of organizations across the Bicol region.

Who We Are

A Water Equipment Supplier based in Naga City, Camarines Sur, Philippines. We are dedicated to providing high-quality water equipment and supplies to meet the needs of Bicolanos.

What We Do

We supply a wide range of water equipment and supplies, including water filtration systems, water dispensers, and other related products. Our goal is to ensure that our customers have access to clean and safe drinking water. We also provide installation and maintenance services for water stations for our clients.
